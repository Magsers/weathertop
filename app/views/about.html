About Weather Top 1000

Weather Top 1000 was created in conjunction with WeatherTop Inc to allow meteorologists to create their own personal accounts where they can input a weather stations and readings for these weather stations. The app does a number of calculations depending on the inputs including the beaufort scale, wind chill, wind direction, minimum and maximum of all the readings and trends in temperature, wind speed and atmospheric pressure. The app displays at the top of your screen the latest conditions from the readings that have been entered.

It is designed so it is easy to use and to navigate. Users can sign up with a unique email address and their own password. From there they can create a weather statation and start adding the latest reading to this station. The app records the time and date of the reading and they are listed accordingly. Their stations which must have a unique name are also listed alphabetically with their latest conditions visible on the dashboard. Users can delete readings or stations.

In customer profile users will have the option to edit personal details on their profile. Unique email addresses must be provided each time. Blank fields will not be accepted when submitting data.

The app is still in development so we will be adding features to this in the near future. Please get in touch if you have any issues with the app or have any suggestions that would ensure a more user friendly version.
